AN ARMY OF TWO

A tiny difference between being the biggest and being the best.

In Etéreo we work with the model of human-centered-design which puts people always at the center of our work. We focus on how we think about design and constantly overhaul our work in order to move away from the ordinary and always stay ambitous.

Besides giving our passion for customer service, we use our knowledge to give life to own projects that fulfil world’s needs, using design and technology as a way to solve problems.

With an army of two people, having the advantage of being compliant in all product dev areas, from A to Z and the help of great professionals in the field of business development, long-term strategy, communication and human relationships.

The things we madly
love doing

  • Strategy

    We help you to properly define what you want to achieve, seeking solutions and improvements outside the initial idea. Turn moving concepts and abstract ideas into tangible structures that quickly familiarize the user as part of his environment.

  • Design

    We facilitate the process of understanding and assimilation of information for users through great design, interaction with users comfortable, simple, intuitive and amazingly beautiful without forgetting effectiveness.

  • Technology

    In a world that never ceases to evolve, we adapt to the learning environment and taking advantage of everything it offers. Through very careful logic, we make coding an art and a thrilling way to give life.

PEOPLE OF ETEREO

The crazy humans behind the screen

Hayder Al-Deen

THE INVETERATE DESIGNER

As Chief Creative Officer, Hayder encapsulates all creative, strategy and product design efforts for Etéreo. Hayder’s combination of design, usability and technology expertise makes Etéreo’s solutions future-proof. Before founding Etéreo, Hayder started his career in advertising, working in agencies and technology studios, where he carried out all kinds of creative and tech projects. After a long period, he started his own path towards freelance, where he offered personalized services to large companies in the advertising sector and was responsible for design and technology implementation at several startups.

hayder@etereo.io

Anthanh Pham

THE NINJA DEVELOPER

As Chief Technical Officer, Anthanh Pham is a polyvalent developer-leader with extensive managing and software development experience in high-growth startups and technology companies in addition to working as a teacher in web technologies. He also collaborates with a research group at the University Rey Juan Carlos in Madrid while working on his doctoral thesis about metaheuristics. Before founding Etéreo, Anthanh worked on several software projects that included both back-end and front-end technologies, challenging himself persistently, with an open, methodical and self-sufficient mind.
