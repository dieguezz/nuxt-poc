The Inveterate Designer

Huibert Evekink

The Thinking Mind

Huibert Jan Evekink spent more than twenty years managing sales, marketing and human resources teams in multinational corporations in the Netherlands, Spain, England and the United States. He is the founder and CEO of Futureteaming and was responsible for the successful “Engage” programme in Amadeus IT Group Spain, training people management to leaders and their teams. He is a recognised coach, speaker and university lecturer on branding, engagement and culture. Huibert holds a master’s degree in Business Law from the University of Leiden in the Netherlands, and he recently completed the Executive Master in Consulting and Coaching for Change degree program at the INSEAD Business School in Paris.
